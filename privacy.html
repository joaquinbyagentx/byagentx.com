Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

At AgentX, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our AI automation services at byagentx.ai.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Name and email address — when you sign up or purchase a plan.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card details.
  • Business details — any context you share during onboarding to help us configure your automation.
  • Usage data — basic analytics about how our website is used (page views, traffic sources), collected anonymously.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your payment and activate your subscription.
  • To contact you for onboarding, setup, and support.
  • To send important account and service updates.
  • To improve our services and website experience.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. When you complete a purchase, your payment details are transmitted directly to Stripe and are never stored on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions: stripe.com/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We share your information only with the service providers necessary to operate our business:

  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Email service providers — to deliver transactional and onboarding emails.

All third-party providers are contractually bound to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period after cancellation, as required for legal, security, and accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. User Data Deletion Instructions

If you want AgentX to delete personal data associated with your account, business onboarding, WhatsApp integration, or Meta-connected experience, you can request deletion at any time.

To request deletion, email us at joaquin@byagentx.ai with the subject line User Data Deletion Request and include:

  • Your name and business name, if applicable.
  • The email address or WhatsApp number associated with your AgentX account or interaction.
  • A short statement that you want your data deleted.

After receiving your request, we will verify the requestor's authority when needed, delete or anonymize personal data that AgentX controls, and confirm completion by email. Some information may be retained only when required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, tax, accounting, or dispute-resolution obligations.

For Meta or WhatsApp related data, this deletion process covers data stored or controlled by AgentX. You may also manage or remove permissions directly through your Meta, Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp account settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions or data deletion requests? Contact us at joaquin@byagentx.ai
